Week 11 | Social Media for SEO & Social Networking

By BriAnna Marsh

I think my business has good potential for social media marketing. I originally was inspired by the small artist community on Twitter and if I can use my site to feature and promote other artists hopefully, I can join the cycle and receive some support myself. I don’t believe social networking can impact my business directly, but I could use some pointers in advertising and creating a flourishing business. I think everyone can use a little push in those areas no matter how big their business is. Overall, my ads didn’t do so hot, I improved from 5 to 7 clicks and reduced the cost of each click which was great but if I wanted to really go hard on an online business there’s still a lot of room for improvement. I took this class to learn these skills and oh boy have I learned a lot; this semester has been great. I don’t have the time or money currently as I am trying to get through school while working two jobs but down the line, I could see this becoming an option for me and I’m super grateful for all I’ve learned even if the stats didn’t come out very pretty.
