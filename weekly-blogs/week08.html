Week 08 | Ads

By BriAnna Marsh

Ads are something I learned a little bit about in a visual design class. That class’s emphasis was on the visual part of course but we did learn a little about typography and the wording of ads. So, while this wasn’t completely new to me, I did get to learn a lot more about the text aspect. It’s a lot more difficult to get your ad to stand out with only text as opposed to interesting visuals. I think I’ve got my options in a good spot. Since my business is called Wholesome Happiness, I did my best to incorporate happiness, smiles, and similar words into my ad options. These broad keywords were mixed into a paragraph explaining that my site features small artists and interesting products that made me happy and might bring joy to someone else. I am struggling a little bit as my theme is very broad but as I collect more interesting artists and affiliate businesses I am finding better keywords and things are getting easier.
