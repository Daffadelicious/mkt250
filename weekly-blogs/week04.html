Week 04 | Site builder & Hosting

By BriAnna Marsh

Site building is my bread and butter! I am currently majoring in Web Development and I absolutely love it. This is the part of the class that I am most proficient in so hopefully my skills here can make up for my lack of business experience. As for site builders, I am most familiar with building sites from raw HTML but I've also used WordPress in the past. For this class, I am going to go with DreamWeaver since I think it looks like a very cool tool and so that I can continue to learn something new in Web Development. As for my host, I will use BlueHost. They have a reasonable price at $2.95 a month for a yearly commitment. Some of the other features they offer is matching $150 for google ads and allowing you to track google analytics. They offer 24/7 support by email or phone and they are compatible with DreamWeaver which is everything I could ask for. I haven't had very many public websites so I'm very excited to see how this turns out and hopefully it will be successful!
