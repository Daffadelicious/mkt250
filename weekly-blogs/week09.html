Week 09 | Relevance and Quality Score

By BriAnna Marsh

So far in my ad campaign I’ve just received an average score. Since I’m new and haven’t given it as much time as I’d like I think that’s decent. I added 20 or so keywords and created tons of headline options with each containing at lest one keyword. I’m also proud of my descriptions as they convey a happy welcoming message with a simple and non-pressuring call to action. I think the best way to improve my score will be adding more niche keywords once I get more affiliate and artist links onto my site. I’m hoping my ad will do well, I already noticed I had 15 or so people view my site before making the ad so that should be a good sign. I’ll see how it performs and scores then go from there. This week was a little frustrating, I felt Google’s own tutorials were out of date, so I had to find different resources or figure it out on my own but in the end, I was able to figure out how to create and connect everything. I accomplished what I needed to despite its difficulty and I’m looking forward to seeing how it pays off score wise.
